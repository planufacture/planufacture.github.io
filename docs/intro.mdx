## Navigation

The main application navigation is organized into modules:

- **Schedule** - Gantt-style timeline for viewing and adjusting work orders
- **Inventory** - Current stock, days cover, and stock projections
- **Materials** - Material availability and requirements
- **Work Orders** - Production orders and their status
- **Capacity Report** - Resource utilization and capacity analysis
- **Demand Forecast** - Forecasted demand for planning
- **Sales Orders** - Customer orders and line items
- **Purchase Orders** - Supplier orders and line items
- **SKUs** - Product definitions and configuration
- **Bill of Materials** - BOM definitions and components
- **Processes** - Production process definitions and routing
- **Resources** - Production resources and capacity settings
- **Availability** - Availability patterns for resources
- **Batch Trace** - Batch and lot traceability

## Key Concepts

- **Work Orders** - Production orders that specify what to make, how much, and when.
- **SKUs** - The items you manufacture, with associated BOMs and processes.
- **Resources** - Production lines, equipment, and capacity constraints.
- **Materials** - Raw materials, ingredients, and packaging components.
- **Scheduling** - Assigning work orders to resources over time.
