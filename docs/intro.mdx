# Getting Started

## Navigation

The main navigation is organised into the following areas:

- **[Schedule](/docs/schedule)** - Timeline for viewing and adjusting work orders
- **[Inventory](/docs/inventory)** - Current stock, days cover, and stock projections
- **[Materials](/docs/materials)** - Material availability and requirements
- **[Work Orders](/docs/work-orders)** - Production orders and their status
- **[Capacity Report](/docs/capacity-report)** - Resource utilisation and capacity analysis
- **[Demand Forecast](/docs/demand-forecast)** - Forecasted demand for planning
- **[Sales Orders](/docs/sales-orders)** - Customer orders and line items
- **[Purchase Orders](/docs/purchase-orders)** - Supplier orders and line items
- **[SKUs](/docs/skus)** - Product definitions and configuration
- **[Bill of Materials](/docs/bill-of-materials)** - BOM definitions and components
- **[Processes](/docs/processes)** - Production process definitions and routing
- **[Resources](/docs/resources)** - Production resources and capacity settings
- **[Availability](/docs/availability)** - Availability patterns for resources
- **[Batch Trace](/docs/batch-trace)** - Batch and lot traceability

## Key Concepts

- **Work Orders** - Production orders that specify what to make, how much, and when.
- **SKUs** - The items you manufacture, with associated BOMs and processes.
- **Resources** - Equipment, machines, and capacity constraints.
- **Materials** - Raw materials, ingredients, and packaging components.
- **Scheduling** - Assigning work orders to resources over time.
